A mark for Openpaper

Five symbol directions + wordmark treatments — each keeps the editorial / paper spirit of the ❦ placeholder, but drawn as a vector you own (crisp at any size, consistent everywhere, trademarkable). Shown big, as a lockup, and at favicon size.

Symbol marks

AThe Fold
Openpaper

A dog-eared page with two lines of writing. The most literal “paper” read — instantly legible, great at tiny sizes. Safe, friendly, unmistakably a publication.

BFleuron
Openpaper

The ❦ reborn as a clean leaf with veins — directly honours the typographic-ornament origin. Most “literary” and distinctive of the set. Coral-forward, elegant.

CThe Caret
Openpaper

A page with a coral text-cursor (I-beam) inside. Says “writing” and quietly nods to the terminal/AI world. Modern, techy, a touch more “tool” than “magazine.”

DThe O
penpaper

The “O” of Openpaper as a ring, with a coral leaf in the counter — monogram + fleuron in one. Doubles as a lockup where the mark is the first letter (“Openpaper”).

ESheets
Openpaper

Two offset sheets — a small “stack of writing.” Reads as paper + collection (a publication, many pieces). Slightly busier at the smallest sizes.

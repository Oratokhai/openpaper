## Competitive Landscape

### General Publication Platforms
| Platform | Strength | Weakness for AI audience |
|---|---|---|
| Substack | Newsletter, monetization, simplicity | Generic, no AI-native features |
| Medium | Discovery, large audience | Declining developer trust |
| dev.to | Developer community, open source | Limited monetization, basic editor |
| Hashnode | Developer-focused, custom domains | Not AI-specific |
| Mirror | Web3/crypto native | Wrong audience |

### AI-Specific Spaces
| Platform | Strength | Weakness |
|---|---|---|
| Hugging Face Blog | Model cards, research | Developer-only, not editorial |
| The Gradient | High quality AI research writing | Not a platform, magazine only |
| Import AI (Jack Clark) | Influential newsletter | One person, not a platform |
| Towards Data Science | Scale, DS/ML community | Dated UX, Medium-dependent |

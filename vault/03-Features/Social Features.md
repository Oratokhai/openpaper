# Social Features

> **Return to:** [[Features]] | [[Home]]

---

## Philosophy

Openpaper has a social layer, but it is **not a social media platform**. Social features exist to serve publishing — discovery, connection, and community around articles — not to create a feed-scrolling habit.

---

## Profile

- Single profile for every user — no distinction between writer and reader
- Public profile showing: published articles, follower count, following count, bio
- Anyone can publish; anyone can read

---

## Engagement

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Likes | One like per user per article |
| Reactions | Multiple reaction types beyond like (TBD — emoji set to define) |
| Comments | On articles |
| Replies | Reply to a specific comment |
| Threads | Nested comment threads |
| Reshares | Reshare another user's article to your own followers |

---

## Network

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Follow | Follow a user to see their new articles in your feed |
| Followers / Following | Counts visible on profile |
| Topics / Tags | Follow a topic (e.g. "prompt engineering") — not just people |
| Activity feed | See what people you follow are liking and commenting on |

---

## Personal

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Saved | Bookmark articles privately (not visible to others) |
| DMs | Direct messages between users |

---

## Notifications

| Trigger | Channel |
|---|---|
| New article from followed writer | In-app + email |
| Comment on your article | In-app |
| Reply to your comment | In-app |
| New follower | In-app |
| Reshare of your article | In-app |
| DM received | In-app |

---

## Trending

- Surfaces top articles across the platform
- Not called "leaderboard" — called **Trending**
- Scope TBD: daily / weekly / all-time

---

## Explicitly Excluded

- Claps / multi-like (Medium-style) — ❌
- Public activity (others can't see your saves) — saves are private

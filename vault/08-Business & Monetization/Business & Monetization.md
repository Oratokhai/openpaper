## Business Model Options

| Model | Description | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|---|
| Free + Premium (reader) | Free reading, paid subscription for extra content | Proven (Substack) | Hard to convert |
| Creator monetization | Platform takes % of creator subscriptions | Aligns incentives | Needs scale |
| Sponsorship / ads | Newsletter sponsorships, job board ads | Immediate revenue | Can feel cheap |
| B2B / Teams | Companies publish & sponsor content | High ARPU | Longer sales cycle |
| Freemium (writer) | Free to publish, paid for analytics/features | Low friction | Monetization lag |

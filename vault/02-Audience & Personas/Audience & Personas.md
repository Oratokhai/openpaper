---

## Primary Audience

AI-enthusiast developers, builders, and users. Anyone who is active in the AI space — building products, writing about models, sharing discoveries, or deeply following AI developments.

**Key principle:** There is no distinction between a "writer" and a "reader" on Openpaper. Everyone has the same single profile. Anyone can write; anyone can read.

---

## Audience Segments

| Segment | Description | They write about... |
|---|---|---|
| AI Builders | Developers building products with AI APIs and models | What they built, how they built it, what broke |
| AI Researchers | People who study, analyze, and write about AI systems | Model behavior, benchmarks, papers, findings |
| AI Power Users | Non-developers who use AI tools deeply and thoughtfully | Tips, workflows, discoveries, comparisons |
| AI Founders | Building startups in the AI space | Company building, GTM, product decisions |
| AI Educators | Teachers, tutorial writers, course creators | Guides, walkthroughs, explainers |
| AI Commentators | Writers covering AI for technical audiences | News, analysis, opinion |

---

## Flywheel

**Writer-first.** Seed the platform with great writers. Readers follow the writers. Growth comes from sharing on LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and word of mouth once the content quality is established.

Launch is open to all — no waitlist, no invite-only.

---

## What They're Missing Today

- A single place that aggregates quality AI writing
- A publication home that feels built *for* them
- A community where AI people gather around writing (not just code repos or social feeds)

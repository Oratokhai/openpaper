# Project Overview

> **Return to:** [[Home]]

---

## What It Is

**Openpaper** is a pure publishing platform built specifically for the AI community — developers, builders, researchers, and enthusiasts who write about what they're building, discovering, and learning in AI.

It is the AI equivalent of what Paragraph.xyz is to the crypto and Web3 community: a single, community-native home where everyone in the space goes to write and read.

## What It Is Not

- Not a social media platform (publishing is the core, social is in service of it)
- Not a portfolio or showcase tool
- Not a generic blogging platform with an AI coat of paint
- Not short-form / feed-based content

---

## The Problem

AI information is deeply fragmented. Great writing about AI lives on LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Substack, YouTube, and personal blogs — with no single unified place to find it all. There is no community-native home for the AI space the way Paragraph.xyz serves Web3.

This means:
- Readers have to follow dozens of scattered sources
- Writers have no dedicated, respected home for AI content
- The community has no central gathering point

## The Solution

Openpaper is that home. One place where AI people write, read, follow each other, and discover everything happening in the space.

---

## Vision

To be the single unified home for the AI writing community — the place where the most important ideas, discoveries, and builds in AI are first published and found.

## Mission

To give AI developers, builders, and enthusiasts the best publishing platform built specifically for them.

---

## Core Principles

1. **Publishing is the core** — everything else serves the act of writing and reading
2. **Writer-first** — attract great writers, readers follow
3. **Community-native** — built for AI people, by AI people
4. **Depth over noise** — articles only, no short-form feed
5. **Open from day one** — no gatekeeping, everyone can join and publish

---

## Closest Comparison

**Paragraph.xyz** — community-native publication platform for Web3/crypto.
Openpaper is that, but for AI.

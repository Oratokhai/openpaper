## Name

**Openpaper**

---

## Why It Works

- "Open" — accessible, community-owned, no gatekeeping, open to all
- "Paper" — publishing, writing, the written word, credibility
- Together: open access to quality writing, for everyone in the AI space
- Clean, two-syllable, memorable, domain-friendly

---

## Brand Direction

*To be defined — but informed by the name:*
- Open, accessible, not elitist
- Serious about writing and depth
- Built for builders — technical credibility without being cold
- Not corporate, not flashy

---

## Brand Voice

*To be defined:*
- Tone: clear, direct, warm — like a smart colleague, not a brand
- How it speaks to writers: "your work belongs here"
- How it speaks to readers: "everything you need, in one place"
